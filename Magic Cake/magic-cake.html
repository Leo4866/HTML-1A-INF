Magic Cake

Magic Cake is one of the tastiest cakers the world has never known. Few have tried it and lived to tell about it, and these are their stories

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 pound of sugar
  • 3 sticks of butter

Directions

  1. mix eggs, sugar and butter in a large bowl
  2. spread into a non-stick dish
  3. bake at 350 degree for 1 hour
  4. let sit at room temperature for 20 minutes
  5. eat and enjoy!
